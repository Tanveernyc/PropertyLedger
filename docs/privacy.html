PropertyLedger Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2026

PropertyLedger is a personal property expense and income tracker. This policy describes what data the app handles and how.

Data we collect

  • Account data: your email address and a password, used solely to sign you in.
  • User content: the properties, expenses, income entries, categories, and notes you create in the app.

How data is used

Your data is used only to provide the app's functionality — storing and displaying your own records. It is linked to your account identity so it can sync to your devices. It is not used for advertising or tracking, is not sold, and is not shared with third parties beyond the infrastructure provider below.

Where data is stored

Data is stored in a Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database (servers in the United States), protected by row-level security so each account can only access its own records. Passwords are hashed; transport is encrypted with TLS.

Data export and deletion

The app includes an "Export All Data" feature that produces a complete JSON and CSV copy of your records at any time. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below.

Analytics and tracking

The app contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and no trackers.
